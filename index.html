The Solution

Teach people how to
love and show up.

Covelature is a date competition app for couples. Each week, partners receive a curated date challenge — a prompt they interpret together and execute on their own terms. When the date is done, they rate each other across five behavioral dimensions. Scores are compared, a wager is settled, and a winner is crowned. The competition makes dating intentional and fun again.

But the app is not the product. The app is the data collection engine.

Every completed date generates 30+ behavioral data points — all voluntarily logged by users who are motivated to be honest because their own growth depends on it. That data flows into a closed-loop AI operating system that runs every layer of the company. No other platform collects data like this. Tinder knows who you swiped. Covelature knows how your date went.

The Five Dimensions

Not a star rating. A behavioral record that travels with you.

Safety
Did both people feel safe and respected? Was the environment emotionally and physically secure?
Presence
Were you fully there — no phones, no distractions, no mental absence?
Authenticity
Did you show up as your real self? No performance, no pretense.
Consideration
Did you think about your partner's experience? The highest form of love is consideration.
Vision
Did the date reflect what you both want long-term? Are you building toward something real?

The AI Operating System

Every action creates an artifact.
Every artifact feeds the system.

The closed-loop AI operating system is the real product. Every decision, every workflow, and every process flows through this intelligent layer that is constantly learning and improving. It is queryable, self-improving, and impossible to replicate without the user base that generates the data.

AI Relationship Coach
Reads each user's behavioral history and delivers personalized feedback tied to their actual dates. Not generic advice. Not a chatbot. A coaching agent that speaks directly to your score patterns over time.
Safety Moderation Layer
Identifies behavioral patterns across the community before harm occurs. Flags bad actors based on score patterns, not self-reports. Protects the singles pool at scale without a massive human moderation team.
Business Intelligence Layer
Dissects aggregated, anonymized venue and behavioral data. Packages insights that restaurants, experience businesses, consumer brands, and relationship researchers will pay for. No individual data is ever sold.
Licensable Infrastructure
As the data compounds and the AI matures, the operating system becomes a licensable product — white-labeled to couples therapy platforms, corporate wellness programs, and any organization that needs to understand how human beings show up for each other.

The Founder

Built from lived experience
nobody else in this space has.

"I picked this idea because I lived the problem from both sides — as someone who failed at relationships and as someone who spent a career watching what happens to people who were never taught how to love."

I built this framework for myself first. My own relationships failed not because I didn't care but because I didn't have the tools. Nobody taught me how to show up. Nobody gave me feedback I could actually use. Nobody held me accountable to the person I wanted to be for someone else. I had to build that understanding through pain.

At the same time I was living that personally, I was watching it professionally on a scale most people never see. In the military I watched what happens to love under pressure. In law enforcement I watched what happens to communities when there is no structure and no standard. In a federal prison I watched men reaching desperately for the relationships they had broken or lost — men who genuinely did not know how to be someone's partner, someone's father, someone's person.

The same failure showing up everywhere. People who want to love. People who don't know how. The framework inside Covelature is not something I read about. It is something I built because I needed it.
